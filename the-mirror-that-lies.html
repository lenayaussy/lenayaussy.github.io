PROJECT 001

The Mirror That Lies

An interactive psychological horror experience exploring identity, self-image, and the systems that shape how we see ourselves.

Overview

The Mirror That Lies is a branching narrative experience built in p5.js. The player explores a haunted bathroom where a mirror reflects different versions of themselves depending on the choices they make. Through interactive storytelling, environmental design, and surreal horror, the project examines perfectionism, comparison, and the pressure to constantly optimize ourselves.

Design Goals

The goal was to create an experience where the player feels the tension between agency and constraint. The mirror acts as both a character and a system: something that reflects the player while also influencing how they see themselves.

Features

• Branching narrative choices
• Interactive mirror environment
• Animated reflection movement
• Atmospheric audio design
• Visual effects and particle systems
